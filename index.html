My Projects

1. Federal Reserve Interest Decisions Prediction

This project explores the predictive power of language by developing a model that forecasts Federal Reserve interest rate decisions based on the textual analysis of Federal Open Market Committee (FOMC) speeches. Advanced NLP techniques are employed to extract key linguistic cues, that signal potential shifts in monetary policy.

2. Detecting Airline Review Discrepancy

This project focuses on identifying and analyzing anomalies in airline customer reviews. By developing a discrepancy model, we aim to detect instances where the expressed sentiment in a review significantly diverges from the assigned star rating. This model could help airlines identify and address areas where customer perceptions and expectations may not be fully reflected in the star rating system.

3. Social Resilience Trajectories in "Suicide Survivors"

Exploring social factors and consecutive mental symptoms of people who experienced the suicide of a close person: is every social intervention equal?

4. Just for Fun: Motorcycle Racing Game in India

Development of a motorcycle racing simulation game utilizing real-world Indian Motorcycle sales data. The project will model motorcycles, riders, and race dynamics, creating a realistic and engaging gaming experience.
